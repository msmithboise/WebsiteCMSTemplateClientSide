March,15th 2021



SubPages!

You now will have the ability to create subpages! Just go into your page settings and on your desired page, select "Add sub page", give it a name and publish it when you're ready. After publishing it, you will see in your navigation bar when hovering over the page. NOTE: You can only create up to five pages deep. An example for that would be "Store" > "Products" > "Fruit" > "Apples" > "Granny Smith Apple".


Developer Notes:

This was a huge goal of mine to be able to offer you all an option to create subpages. We recognize that most thriving websites contained this feature and we wanted your websites to be no different. So after (many) hours of working at it, I feel like this will take your websites to a whole new level.

Side Nav bar added!

You may have noticed that we changed the mobile navigation menu. This was at first to create a structure for the subpages to work in, however we like it so much we decided to keep it on the desktop as a side navigation bar. Just click on the icon in the upper left hand corner of the screen.


Developer Notes:

As I said, I was originally working on the subpages and how I could allow visitors to easily navigate through the sub pages, I found this side nav feature to be a great solution and addition to the web building platform.

And finally...Animation!

You now have the ability to create new animations for your images and text. You will see in the styling section a new tab called "pre built animations" This is for your convenience. Simply click on an animation preset and see how it transforms your text or images. Here's something to try: Add a hyperlink to an image, set it to 400x400px and add the animation "Pulse". You've just created a very cool button.


Developer Notes:

This was another desire of mine, to make your pages more fluid and come alive for your web page visitors. This was also alot of fun to browse through the individual animations and get new ideas of how to transform your websites into an experience your visitors will be telling their friends about.

This concludes our update.

Enjoy the new features! There are alot more of them in the works!


-Mike
